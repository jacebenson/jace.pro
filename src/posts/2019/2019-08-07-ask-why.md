There are problems we should be solving, but they're not part of any requirements. That's where you come in. Use your professional skills to help us shape a solution that will make our customers happy, without breaking the bank.

I've been on most sides of the equation when it comes to this. I started as a help desk technician. Then I tried to help define the requirements that we needed. I've been a consultant where I fulfilled the requirements. Now I'm a customer again. When you're a customer, you don't care how it works, just that it works. At least that was my opinion back then.
